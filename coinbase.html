Overview

This is a portfolio-building project to step out of my usual box of SaaS logistics applications and design a new feature for an existing mobile application. I myself am a user of Coinbase and have had a desire for a feature where I could communicate with other Coinbase users to better understand market sentiment.

Goal

If this were a real project, the goal would be to provide a community where Coinbase users can connect with each other regarding an asset of their interest, resulting in increased time spent by users viewing a particular asset and an increase in the likelihood of them executing more transactions on Coinbase.

Personas

Personas for this project are loosely based around myself and friends who are retail crypto investors that utilize Coinbase as their main exchange. Even for this simple portfolio project, defining personas helped keep focus on priorities for the design.


The key factors to capture for personas were:

  • Crypto investment styel or strategy
  • Frequency of referencing a particular asset on Coinbase
  • Where and how do they consume news or information about crypto

Wireframes

Using Figma, I created low-fidelity wireframes. The low-fidelity wireframes were used for gathering initial feedback as a proof of concept to the users, as well as to plan out a general idea for the solution.


User Feedback

Users were overall content with the first iteration and felt that the concept would be viable. Criticism of the sentiment buttons in the post drawer was raised.

UI Design

This project was a good example of my abilities to work within an already defined design system. I recreated a mini design kit for things that would be necessary to complete the UIs.



These high fidelity mockups were then shown to users for final feedback. Users felt the design fit seamlessly into the Coinbase experience.

Some users mentioned that it would be nice to implement features like gif and image sharing as well. If this were a real project, these could be considered post-MVP features to add.
