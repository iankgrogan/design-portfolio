About

Hey, I'm Ian

I'm a user experience product designer based in South Carolina, living with my wife and our daughter.

I enjoy designing digital solutions that improve people's lives, jobs, and experiences. Solving problems scratches the itch for the left side of my brain — and doing so through visually appealing designs gives the right side the warm and fuzzies.

My strong suits are building mutually beneficial working relationships with end users and creating logical UI flows based on their needs. I also integrate AI heavily into my design process — using tools like Figma Make, Claude, and Copilot to move faster, stay organized, and spend more time on the work that matters.

Background

Creativity has always been the thread.

A bit of a long story, but I've always had a desire and drive to be creative. From an early age I wanted to be creative through music, and at the age of seven I learned to play bass guitar and joined a band (yes, it was as cringe as you might think it was).

In high school and going into college, I developed a strong passion for photography and videography. At the peak of that pursuit, I was able to film professional motocross riders and races, as well as college football and basketball games.

I graduated college with a major in economics and a minor in finance. At my first place of employment, there was an opportunity (a total void) for UI design. Having photo and video editing skills, I started out utilizing primitive tools to make UI layouts. As you can imagine, I quickly learned there is more to UI design than a pretty layout. This is when I started to really learn about the total UX design process and gain experience implementing it. Fast forward, and I've been full-time designing for the past 4–5 years, improving with every new challenge.
